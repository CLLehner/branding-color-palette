Color Usage Guidelines

Primary Branding
Use Oat Milk and Driftwood as your primary brand colors for logos, headers, and main elements. These create trust and sophistication.
Fashion Products
Soft pastels work beautifully for clothing lines, especially Vanilla Cream and Soft Blush for feminine pieces.
Digital Presence
Use the neutral foundation as backgrounds with California Warmth colors for call-to-action buttons and highlights.
Packaging
Combine Linen backgrounds with Golden Hour accents for premium, sustainable packaging that photographs beautifully.
Typography
Use Charcoal for primary text, Driftwood for secondary text, and Pure White for text on darker backgrounds.
Lifestyle Content
Palm Tree and Terracotta add natural, earthy touches perfect for lifestyle photography and wellness content.

This palette embodies the effortless elegance of California living – where luxury feels accessible, wellness is a priority, and every color tells a story of sun-soaked serenity and mindful living.

It is designed to work across fashion, digital platforms, packaging, and lifestyle content while maintaining that effortless luxury feel. Each color has been carefully named to evoke the lifestyle (Malibu Sand, Golden Hour) and comes with specific usage guidelines for different brand applications.

This palette would work beautifully for any lifestyle, jewelry, apparel, and/or accessories brand targeting an aesthetic called "California Soft Life" - sophisticated enough for luxury positioning, but warm and approachable enough to feel accessible.
